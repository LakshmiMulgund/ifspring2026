Development

Wandersong is an independent game developed by Greg Lobanov and released in 2018. Created with a small team, the game focuses on creativity, storytelling, and emotion.

Approach

No combat-based gameplay
Music as primary interaction
Focus on exploration

Core Mechanic

Color-based music wheel
Used to solve puzzles
Interacts with environment

Game Identity

Bright, bold color palette
Simple geometric shapes
Layered environments
Optimism and perseverance
Non-traditional heroism
Connection through music

Creative Direction by Emily Ellison | Designed by Lakshmi Mulgund
