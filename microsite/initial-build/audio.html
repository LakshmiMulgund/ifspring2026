About Development Audio

Audio

Music is central to Wandersong’s gameplay and storytelling. The soundtrack reflects the emotional tone of each moment, shifting between playful, reflective, and dramatic.

Sound Design

The music for Wandersong was composed by A Shell in the Pit, known for expressive and dynamic game soundtracks.

Instead of background music, the soundtrack is integrated into gameplay. Players use singing to interact with the environment.

The audio design supports emotional shifts between lighthearted exploration and serious narrative moments.
